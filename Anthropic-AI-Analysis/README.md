# Anthropic AI: Growth & Market Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Anthropic's growth trajectory and market positioning in the highly competitive generative AI sector. It serves as a competitive benchmarking tool, translating complex market data into clear insights regarding Anthropic's standing against other major AI models and tech giants.

## Key Insights Explored
* **Market Share & Adoption:** How fast is Anthropic capturing enterprise and developer market share compared to industry baselines?
* **Feature & Capability Benchmarking:** Visualizing the comparative strengths of Anthropic's models (e.g., context window size, reasoning capabilities) against competitors.
* **Funding & Valuation Trajectory:** Mapping the correlation between strategic investments and subsequent market growth.

## Technical Details
* **Software:** Tableau Desktop
* **File Type:** Unpackaged `.twb` (Source structure) / Packaged `.twbx` (Includes data)
* **To View:** Download the `.twbx` file and open it using [Tableau Reader](https://www.tableau.com/products/reader) or Tableau Desktop.
